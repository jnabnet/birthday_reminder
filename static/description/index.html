Odoo 9: Birthday Reminder



This module automatically sends an email to customer on thier Birthdays.


How It Works




  • Date of Birth Field : You have to set Birthdate of Customer here in Customer's View.




  • Scheduler Configuration: You can set the time as you wish for am email to be sent to Customer.




  • Email: This is how an Email will be generated to sent greetings to Customer.
